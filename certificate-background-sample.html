Certificate Background Template

Dimensions: 1754 × 1240 px (A4 Landscape at 150 DPI)

How to use:

  1. Remove the instructions overlay
  2. Take a screenshot of this page (full certificate area)
  3. Or use browser DevTools to capture as PNG
  4. Upload as background in your certificate editor

Press F12 → Console → Run: document.querySelector('.instructions').remove()
